Le ski

Le ski

I live near Annecy and its many mountains since I was born. That’s why I ski since my three years and why I really enjoy that sport. I have begun the ski with my parents and after I have learned with a ski instructor. Then, I have joined a ski competition club where I practiced two disciplines, the Slalom and the Giant. I improved myself a lot during that time. Finally, I turned into freeride skiing where I can do more as I want and express myself with the ski. Due to that disciplines, I am able to ski in different types of slopes and snows. One of the best advantages about freeride is that gives me access to inaccessible places and landscapes.

Le basket

Le basket

I watch basketballs games of the NBA league for a few years, especially those of my favorite team : Golden State Warriors. I was so proud of them when I watched them winning the last season league. I’ve always wanted to play basketball but I didn’t have the time for during my high school years. That bothered me particularly because really enjoyed that beautiful sport. Finally, by joining the “IUT” I noticed that the school offered a basketball section for beginners and others, and I decided to register. Then after several trainings I realized that I love to watch and practice basketball. I am always practicing basketball with the IUT and I am decided to continue as long as I will be able to.

La guitare

La guitare

Finally, I particularly like listening music and that’s why I wanted to learn a musical instrument. I went towards the guitar, and I learned it alone with guitar tabs and youtube video. I decided later to take some lesson and during one year I have learned the same instrument but in another “squarer” way, starting by learning to read notes and only after to play them. Then I realized that learning method was not that really interested me about playing the guitar. I preferred to learn on my own music pieces that I wanted. Today, I continue to play the guitar for me and as I want. Playing that instrument allow me to calm down and forget the rest.
